Meron

A private run & lift dashboard

Meron is a hybrid training dashboard — built for athletes who run and lift, where no existing tool does both well. It pulls your endurance work (rides, runs, FIT files) and your strength training into one view: routes on a map, training-load curves, race predictions, lift-PR progression, and a chat widget that answers questions across both sides of your training.

It started because the existing options forced you to pick one sport: an endurance silo (Strava) or a lifting silo (Hevy, Strong). Meron treats your training as one program. Everything runs locally on your own server; the data stays yours.
