Athenaeum

A self-hosted reading library

Athenaeum is the shelf of books I’ve read and own, browsable publicly. It tracks reading status (audiobook / physical / ebook), groups books by series, syncs covers and metadata from Hardcover, and pushes my library out to Kobo / KOReader over OPDS so I can pull a book onto an e-reader without re-uploading anything.
