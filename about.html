About

[Draft bio — edit freely.] I'm a senior machine-learning engineer in Denver working on intelligent sensing — detection, classification, and geolocation across RF, vision, and audio.

I like problems where the messy physical world meets software: turning raw signals, video, and sensor data into something a model — and a person — can actually use. Most of what I build lives close to hardware, from software-defined radios to small edge devices in the field.
