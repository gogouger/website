Meron

A private cross-training dashboard — running & lifting

Meron turns raw Strava exports into something legible: routes on a map, training trends over time, race predictions, strength progression, and a chat widget that answers questions over your own ride and run data.

It started as a way to make sense of years of FIT files without handing them to a third party. Everything runs locally; the data stays yours.
